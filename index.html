MakerBot 3D Printer Tutorial





Step 1:

  • First you need a file to print, if you don't have your own file you can download this example.


    • Step 2:

  • Once you have your desired file go to cloudprint.makerbot.com/print.
  • On the website, you will see a bar at the top of the page that looks something like this:

  • In this menu, you should select the model of Printer that you are going to use.


    • Step 3:

  • After the correct settings are selected you can now insert your model file using the upload button as shown in the picture below.


    • Step 4:

  • After uploading your file click the red EXPORT button in the top right corner of the website that looks like this:

  • This will might take a moment to export, once its done exporting it will download a file.


    • Step 5:

  • After downloading this file, transfer it to a usb drive.
